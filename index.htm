Cats

Introduction

Cats are adorable animals that have been domesticated for thousands of years. They make great pets and are loved by millions of people around the world.

Breeds

  • Siamese
  • Persian
  • Maine Coon
  • Sphynx

Fun Facts

  • Cats can jump up to six times their body length in a single bound.
  • Cats spend up to 50% of their waking hours grooming themselves.
  • The world's oldest cat lived to be 38 years old.
